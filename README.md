# 🧠 Project: AdhereX AI

AdhereX AI is a predictive healthcare platform designed to address one of healthcare’s biggest challenges: long-term treatment non-adherence among chronic disease patients.

The platform detects **Biopsychosocial Drift** — measurable declines in sleep, activity, mood, and medication adherence patterns that occur before patients abandon treatment.

Using predictive analytics, emotion-aware AI interaction, and a real-time Digital Twin visualization, AdhereX AI helps patients stay engaged while enabling caregivers to intervene early.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- Predictive behavioral drift detection
- 14-day abandonment risk forecasting
- Emotion-aware AI support system
- 3D Digital Twin health visualization
- Adaptive reminder engine
- Smart caregiver escalation
- Real-time health stability dashboard

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Chronic disease patients often fail to maintain long-term treatment consistency due to behavioral, emotional, and lifestyle factors.

AdhereX AI aims to proactively identify early warning signs of treatment abandonment and support both patients and caregivers through intelligent intervention systems.
